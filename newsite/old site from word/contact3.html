Welcome to Wesley Chapel United Methodist Church

 

We have it going on!  We are a Christ-centered community dedicated to making disciples.  How do we do this, you might ask?  We love God, fully trusting God with our lives, living a new and transformed life by grace through faith.  We also love and care for others as we share what Jesus Christ has done for us in our lives and by showing them deeds of mercy and compassion. 

 

This site reflects who we are and what we are like.  We hope that you like what you see and that you will come and visit us in the wonderful village of Misenheimer, NC.  We hope that you will begin to see why we love to call Wesley Chapel our church home.  Maybe you’ll consider making Wesley Chapel UMC your “home” too! 

 

Look around this site to find out more about our people, our history, our ministries and outreach, and our activities.  If you have any questions or comments, or want to tell us about yourself, please email us.
